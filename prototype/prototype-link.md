## 🛠️ Testing Instructions
1. **Browse & Search:** Search for items (e.g., "Holy Basil" or "Boba") or filter using category pills (`Thai Dishes`, `Beverages`).
2. **Order & Basket:** Click **Add ฿55** or **Add ฿45** to add items to your campus order basket.
3. **Checkout:** Click **Confirm Order & Pay** inside the basket drawer.
4. **Vendor Admin Mode:** Click **Switch to Vendor Admin** on the left navigation bar to open the **Kitchen Management Operations Portal** and monitor live orders and runner dispatches.
