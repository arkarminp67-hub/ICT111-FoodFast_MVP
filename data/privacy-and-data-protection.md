# Privacy and Data Protection Policy — FoodFast

## 1. Data Collection & Purpose
FoodFast collects minimal personally identifiable information (PII) required to facilitate quick canteen pre-ordering and pickup:
* **Student Identifier & Name:** Used strictly to confirm identity at the canteen counter upon order retrieval.
* **Phone Number:** Collected to send real-time notifications (SMS/Push) when an order is ready.
* **Transactional History:** Tracks meal selections to generate canteen demand insights.

## 2. Protection & Governance
* **Data Minimization:** Financial details (credit cards/bank accounts) are never stored locally on FoodFast servers.
* **Encryption Standard:** All client-server communications use TLS 1.3 encryption.
* **User Rights:** Students retain the right to clear their transactional history or request full account removal at any point during the term.
