# Critical Assumptions — FoodFast

This document outlines and ranks the core assumptions behind the FoodFast ecosystem by risk and current evidence level.

| ID | Category | Assumption Statement | Risk Level (1-5) | Evidence Level (1-5) | Validation Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ASM-01** | User Problem | Students are willing to use a digital web application rather than queuing in physical canteen lines. | 5 (High) | 2 (Low) | Interactive usability test tasks. |
| **ASM-02** | Usability | Campus food vendors can successfully navigate their dashboard to update order status parameters. | 4 (High) | 1 (Very Low) | Simulated merchant order-management run. |
| **ASM-03** | Technical | The order flow can process state updates (Pending -> Preparing -> Ready) without lagging. | 4 (High) | 2 (Low) | Multi-step interactive script run. |
| **ASM-04** | Value | Students are willing to pay a small convenience fee for delivery or priority pick-up services. | 5 (High) | 1 (Very Low) | Exit interview value-perception survey. |

*Note: Risk scale ranges from 1 (lowest danger to MVP) to 5 (highest danger of system failure). Evidence scale ranges from 1 (no proof/guesswork) to 5 (strongly validated by earlier data).*
