# Experiment Script — FoodFast

This script guides the facilitator and the tester through the MVP evaluation without introducing leading bias.

## 1. Facilitator Protocol
* **Introduction:** "Thank you for participating. We are testing a campus food pre-ordering app called FoodFast. Please think out loud as you interact with the screens. We are testing the software, not your ability."
* **Rule:** Do not intervene unless the user is completely stuck. Note all hesitation points.

## 2. Test Scenarios and User Tasks
* **Task 1 (Browsing):** Navigate to "Canteen Stall A" and find the "Spicy Basil Chicken Rice".
* **Task 2 (Checkout):** Add the meal to your cart, proceed to checkout, and complete the simulated payment action[cite: 2].
* **Task 3 (Tracking):** Open your active order status page and identify the stage of your meal preparation[cite: 2].
* **Task 4 (Merchant State change):** (Admin-side test) Switch views to the merchant dashboard and update order status to "Ready for Pickup"[cite: 2].
