# Product Concept: FoodFast MVP (RSU Campus)

### 1. Target User
- Rangsit University (RSU) students and campus staff who have tight schedules between lectures, short lunch breaks, or exam study blocks.

### 2. Problem Statement
- Extreme high-density foot traffic at campus dining hubs (such as the Building 6 Canteen) during peak lunch hours results in long ordering queues, causing students to skip meals or arrive late to classes.

### 3. Proposed System
- A web-based single-page pre-ordering platform allowing campus users to aggregate meal items from verified university food court stalls and track deliveries directly to localized drop-off nodes.

### 4. Value Proposition
- "Exam Prep Fuel Station" — skip the standard cafeteria lines entirely by selecting pre-determined campus drop-off zones and receiving fresh delivery on-site.

### 5. MVP Scope (In-Scope)
- Dual login/signup flow using authentic university emails (`@rsu.ac.th`).
- Multi-stall catalog browsing with real-time text query and category pill filters (Thai Dishes, Beverages, Bakery).
- Interactive sliding basket drawer tracking individual item counts and pricing.
- Route tracking visualization module responding to localized drop-off offset variables.

### 6. Out-of-Scope Items
- Live external GPS positioning via heavy map rendering engines.
- True credit card or banking API gateway integration (simulated order completion only for MVP scope).
