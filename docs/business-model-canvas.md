# Business Model Canvas — FoodFast

## 1. Customer Segments
* University students looking for quick, affordable campus meals.
* Campus vendors and canteens wanting to increase order volume and streamline queues.
* Campus delivery riders (students earning extra pocket money between classes).

## 2. Value Propositions
* **For Students:** Avoid long canteen lines; pre-order food for fast pickup or fast on-campus delivery.
* **For Vendors:** Better order management dashboard, reduced physical crowd congestion, and increased sales reach.

## 3. Channels
* FoodFast Web Application (Mobile-responsive UI).
* Campus student community group chats (Telegram / WhatsApp).
* QR codes placed on canteen tables and notice boards.

## 4. Customer Relationships
* Automated self-service ordering system.
* Real-time order status tracking (Received -> Preparing -> Ready/In-Transit).
* In-app support/feedback channel for order disputes.

## 5. Revenue / Sustainability
* Small convenience or platform fee per delivery order.
* Premium visibility tier for featured campus food vendors.
* Operational efficiency value for the university by optimizing campus canteen traffic.

## 6. Key Resources
* Web application platform and active database tracking order states.
* Network of registered campus food stalls and vendors.
* Active pool of student delivery riders.

## 7. Key Activities
* Maintaining and updating the food ordering application platform.
* Processing orders and handling secure transaction routing.
* Onboarding new campus food vendors and verifying student riders.

## 8. Key Partners
* Campus Administration and Facilities Management.
* University Canteen Vendors / Stall Owners.
* Student Council / Student Delivery Association.

## 9. Cost Structure
* Application hosting fees and domain costs.
* Marketing and promotional materials (QR flyers across campus).
* Development time, operational support, and rider payout structures.
