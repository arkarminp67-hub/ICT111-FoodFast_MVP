# Feedback Questionnaire — FoodFast

Please ask the testers the following neutral questions immediately after completing the script[cite: 2].

## 1. Quantitative Rating Questions (Scale 1 to 5)
* **Q1:** How straightforward was it to locate your desired meal items on the menu interface? *(1 = Very Confusing, 5 = Extremely Clear)*[cite: 2]
* **Q2:** How would you rate the experience of completing the cart checkout process? *(1 = Frustrating, 5 = Very Effortless)*[cite: 2]
* **Q3:** How helpful was the live order tracking view in keeping you informed of your food status? *(1 = Not Helpful, 5 = Extremely Helpful)*[cite: 2]

## 2. Qualitative Open-Ended Questions
* **Q4:** What particular aspect of the order creation workflow did you find most confusing or slow?[cite: 2]
* **Q5:** If you could change one specific behavior on the tracking interface, what would it be?[cite: 2]
* **Q6:** Under what circumstances would you choose to use this app instead of waiting in line at the university canteen?[cite: 2]
