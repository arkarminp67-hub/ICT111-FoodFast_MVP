# MVP Strategic Decision — FoodFast

Our validation metrics collected from 20 target users have provided a clear, empirical direction for our development team[cite: 5].

## Strategic Verdict: REVISE AND PERSEVERE

### Justification
1. **Strong Core Validation:** An **80% Task Success Rate** and an **Average Interest Level of 4.15/5** prove that students actively care about solving canteen queues using FoodFast[cite: 5].
2. **Defined Revision Plan:** Rather than changing the business model entirely, we will address our key confusion points directly[cite: 5]:
   * **Simplify Checkout (FR-03):** Reduce mandatory input fields during ordering[cite: 5].
   * **Rephrase Progress States (FR-05):** Change complex statuses to simpler tags like "Preparing" and "Ready for Pick-up"[cite: 5].
