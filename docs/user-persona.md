# Lab 04: User Persona - FoodFast

## 1. Persona Profile: Mi Su Zin Swar (The Time-Constrained Student)
* **Age:** 21
* **Role:** 3rd Year Information Technology (IT) Undergrad Student
* **User Type:** Primary Consumer (App User)
* **Archetype:** The back-to-back lecture schedule rusher.

---

## 2. Behavioral Characteristics & Context
Mi Su Zin Swar has tightly packed IT university lecture blocks. On typical high-traffic days, her afternoon break slots allow a very short transitional gap to travel between lecture rooms, purchase a meal, eat, and arrive punctually at her next lab session.

---

## 3. Goals & Motivations
* **Primary Goal:** Secure a fresh lunch quickly during peak hours (11:30 AM – 1:30 PM) without standing in slow, static lines.
* **Secondary Goal:** Maximize her short break duration for group coordination and assignments rather than wasting it standing in a queue.

---

## 4. Validated Pain Points (Backed by Lab 03 Evidence)
* **The 10-Minute Line Barrier:** Regularly gets stuck waiting 11 to 20 minutes physically in front of popular campus food stalls (Validated by 80% of our Lab 03 survey respondents).
* **Academic Disruption:** Has experienced severe schedule delays and has had to rush or skip meals entirely to avoid arriving late to her afternoon classes due to manual vendor ticket delays (Validated by 72.5% of Lab 03 survey respondents).

---

## 5. Inefficient Workarounds
* Sends one designated friend or teammate early to stand in line and order for the whole group, causing confusion at the vendor counter.
* Defaults to nutrient-poor vending machine snacks or convenience food simply to bypass the main cafeteria rush.
