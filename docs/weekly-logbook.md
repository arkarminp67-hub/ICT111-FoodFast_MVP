### Selected Opportunity
FoodFast: Smart Vendor Pre-Ordering Application

### Why We Selected It
Our customer discovery data proved that saving the 11–20 minute queue window is the most useful value proposition for our users.

### What We Rejected
We rejected adding complex physical order-pickup lockers or sensor systems because our data shows that users simply want a clear digital order status screen, keeping our development highly feasible without IoT.

### What We Learned
1. Never ask leading questions that pitch the solution too early; focus entirely on past user behavior.
2. 72.5% of our campus peers skip meals due to queues, making this a high-impact problem to solve.
3. Data patterns should drive your feature priority list, such as focusing on mobile wallet fields first.
